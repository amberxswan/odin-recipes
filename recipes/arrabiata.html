Penne Arrabbiata

This spicy Italian Penne Arrabbiata is homemade in 20 minutes! it makes a great vegetarian dish, served with fresh parmesan, basil, and parsley.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne rigate
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (or to taste depending how spicy you like it)
  • 1 can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 6 fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup fresh chopped parsley

Method

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water, according to package instructions

  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and crushed red pepper; cook, stirring for 30 seconds

  3. Add tomatoes, crushing them roughly with the back of a wooden spoon, and tomato paste

  4. Bring to a simmer over low heat and cook for 5-10 minutes. Remove from heat and add fresh chopped basil

  5. When pasta is cooked, drain the water and add it to the sauce. Toss well. Taste and add more seasoning if needed

  6. Serve immediately, topped with a generous portion of the grated cheese and fresh chopped parsley
